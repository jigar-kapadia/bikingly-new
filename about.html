ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Traffic-free roads, healthier people, and a more sustainable planet — that's what we want to create with ours bike sharing service (and of course, do it profitably!). We're a group of friends who want to offer a mode of transportation to individuals and businesses that is affordable, healthy, and green.

We've commuted to both school and work all our lives and have seen it all — inconvenient rickshaws that don't always go where you want, crowded buses that drain you before you even get to work, and unreliable cabs that are too expensive for short, daily commutes.

We want the citizens of tomorrow to have better options to get where they want to, when they want to, without having to wait. To achieve that, we've developed an innovative business model powered by our easy-to-use app and special bicycles that you can take to go where you need to, to do what you want to.

And if you're anything like us, we know you want to do a lot of things. So why wait for anyone or anything else, when you can ride away, right away with Bikingly?
